Team introduction
  • Hushnow，In Chinese idiom, it can be understood as the meaning of "all music is silent", which mainly expresses a harmonious and calm atmosphere. Our group chose "Hush-now" as the name of the group mainly because we hope to create a harmonious and calm atmosphere within the group, so that the members can always remain calm and rational when discussing and cooperating with each other in the face of difficulties and challenges.
